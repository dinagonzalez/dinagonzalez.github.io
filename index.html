In the land of garlic, Gilroy California, there is a hidden treasure, The Pepper Plant Sauces and Seasoning. The Pepper Plant is locally owned by the Wagner family since 1933. The Original California Style Hot Pepper Sauce is my favorite hot sauce that compliments just about anything. If you want to turn up the flavor on your next meal, The Pepper Plant Sauces and Seasoning is a life saver. You can sprinkle it on chicken, ribs, eggs, chili beans or anything you want to add great flavor to. I have created six simple, savory dishes using The Pepper Plant California Style Hot Pepper Sauce and Seasoning. Enjoy!
